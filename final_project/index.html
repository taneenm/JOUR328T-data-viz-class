Gender Expression, Genres and Goodreads

Taneen Momeni
4 May 2022

Since 2006, book fanatics across the world have been tracking their reading progress, reviewing books and following their favorite authors on Goodreads. In addition to tracking individual readers information, the social cataloging platform also tracks the performance of books and authors. I took a deeper dive into the 15 most popular books published each year and explored the gender expressions of the authors, what genres each of the 165 fit into and their.

I decided to study the gender expression, more specifically the pronouns, of these authors since the genders of all of them were not availble online, and pronouns do not equal gender. Additionally, I thought it would be interesting to see how common it was to use neutral or different pronouns, as it is a point of contention in the traditional literary community.
